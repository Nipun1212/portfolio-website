Introducing Memoir: My Exciting Django-Powered Social Media Project!
I'm thrilled to share my latest project, Memoir, a social media app with a unique twist! Memoir is all about collaboration and shared experiences. I'm developing a special feature that allows users to create collaborative accounts called "Memoirs."
Imagine planning events, trips, or projects with friends, family, or teammates, and having a dedicated space to share memories together. With Memoir, users can create new Memoirs, invite others to join, and have full control over the posts and their visibility.
Each Memoir acts like a private group, where members can post text, images, videos, and more. It's a place to connect, interact, and engage with content that's meaningful to the group. I'm excited to explore the possibilities of user roles within Memoirs. Administrators can manage the Memoir, moderators can curate content, and members can actively contribute. It's about empowering the users to shape their collective experiences.
Follow along on my GitHub repository to see the code and contribute to the journey of Memoir's creation.
